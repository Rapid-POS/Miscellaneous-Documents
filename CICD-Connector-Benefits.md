# Rapid CI/CD Connector Benefits

_Updated June 4, 2026_

**CI/CD** stands for **Continuous Integration / Continuous Deployment**.

Starting in 2024, Rapid began migrating connectors (integrations) to a CI/CD pipeline. This approach allows our connectors to receive ongoing updates automatically, helping to keep them current and reliable.

### What This Means for You

With a CI/CD connector, you receive:

- Automatic bug fixes
- Automatic feature enhancements
- Updates in response to third-party platform changes
- Reduced downtime and maintenance requests
- Ongoing improvements without requiring manual reinstallation
- Predictable monthly pricing

### How It Worked Previously

In the past, when a new feature was developed or a bug was fixed, the connector had to be manually reinstalled for each client that requested the update. This process required labor billed at our standard hourly rate.

As a result, clients often experienced unexpected costs and delays while updates were rolled out individually.

### How CI/CD Improves the Experience

With CI/CD, connector updates are deployed automatically. New features, bug fixes, performance improvements, and compatibility updates are delivered without requiring a manual reinstall.

In many cases, issues can be resolved before you ever experience them.

#### Why This Matters

Third-party platforms regularly make changes to their systems, APIs, security requirements, and functionality. These changes can sometimes affect integrations.

For example, if Shopify, BigCommerce, ShipStation, Mailchimp, or another integrated platform changes its API, Rapid can deploy compatibility updates automatically before the change impacts your business.

This helps reduce downtime, minimize disruption, and ensure uninterrupted operations.

### Supporting Continuous Improvement

CI/CD connectors are actively maintained products rather than one-time software installations.

Your subscription helps fund:

- Ongoing connector maintenance, bug fixes, and performance improvmeents
- Compatibility updates for third-party platforms
- New conenctor features and functionality
- Infrastructure required for automated deployments

This approach allows Rapid to continually improve the connector while ensuring all clients benefit from enhancements as they become available.

### Deployment Notifications and Release Notes

Rapid will attempt to provide at least 24 hours' notice prior to scheduled connector upgrades, along with an estimated deployment window. In rare circumstances, an important update may need to be deployed on short notice or without notice.

Documentation and historical release notes for our CI/CD connectors are available on GitHub: https://github.com/Rapid-POS
